The early life of George S. Patton

George S. Patton was born in 1885 inSan Gabriel,Californiato George S. Patton II (born George William Patton but changed his middle name to Smith in 1868), an attorney, and Ruth Wilson. Ruth was the daughter if Benjamin Davis Wilson, the second mayor of Los Angeles and a Ranchero. Patton's geneology can be traced to England, Scotland, Ireland, France and Wales. Patton believed in reincarnation, and had stated he had previously fought in battles and wars in his past life.Patton's family had already had a long list of members that where in several wars which inspired him to become a war hero himself. In 1910, Patton married Beatrice Ayer, a childhood friend, they had three children: Beatrice, Ruth, and George IV ( who also became a major general in the United States army and served during the Korean and Vietnam wars.)
