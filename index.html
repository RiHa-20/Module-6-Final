An introduction to George S. Patton

George S. Patton was a well-known U.S. Army General best known for his service in World War 2 and the Allied invasion of Normandy. There is a lot of interesting information about General Patton that I will be sharing on this site. I will mainly be focusing on his early life and his military career. Patton is well-known around the world and you may have heard of him before. But there is still a lot of interesting information about this man and some mysterious causes around his death, to learn more about George S. Patton click through my site.

The Most Audacious General

Patton was known after his death as the most Audacious General, which has led to some conspiracies around his death seeing as there was some strange things that happend days before and after his death. This information will be covered in the "General Patton's Military career" section of this website.
